A specialty built on real experience

Tax Uncomplicated, the American headquarters, and Klemsen Consulting, the local Korean branch office, were founded to help US citizens and residents living abroad navigate their tax obligations with confidence and ease.

Having lived overseas for more than 17 years, Katie has developed deep expertise in preparing tax returns for expats — including filing Foreign Bank Account Reports (FBARs) and ensuring full compliance with the Foreign Account Tax Compliance Act (FATCA).

A number of tax law changes, including Affordable Care Act provisions and 2018 reforms, can be complicated. Whether you're a first-time filer, live abroad, or have a complex tax situation, you need a preparer who is genuinely familiar with your circumstances.

Katie always gives reasonable, upfront quotes — no surprises. She can help regardless of how complicated your situation or how long it's been since you've filed.
